Commerce, CA

Commerce, CA trucking & logistics headquarters.

TCI Transportation's Commerce CA facility serves as the company's corporate headquarters and a major trucking and logistics hub for the Los Angeles area. The full-service location provides commercial truck leasing, truck rental, contract maintenance, dedicated transportation services, and used truck sales. The Commerce terminal also features a 26,000-square-foot body shop capable of handling virtually any commercial truck body repair. Conveniently located in central Los Angeles just off Interstate 5 and only half a mile south of the 710 Freeway, the facility supports transportation operations throughout Southern California.
