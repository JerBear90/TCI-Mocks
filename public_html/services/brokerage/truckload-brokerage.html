Truckload brokerage services that move your freight with confidence

Access reliable full truckload capacity across the country through TCI Transportation's brokerage division. From dry van and temperature-controlled to flatbed and expedited shipments, our carrier network and logistics expertise ensure your freight arrives on time, every time.

What Are Truckload Brokerage Services?

Truckload brokerage services connect shippers who need to move full trailer loads of freight with qualified carriers who have available capacity. As a licensed freight broker, TCI acts as the intermediary — sourcing the right carrier for each shipment based on equipment type, lane expertise, transit time requirements, and rate competitiveness.

TCI's brokerage division maintains relationships with thousands of vetted carriers across all equipment types: 53-foot dry vans, temperature-controlled reefers, step-deck and flatbed trailers, and specialized equipment for oversized or high-value loads. Whether you're shipping a single load or managing hundreds of shipments per week, TCI's truckload brokerage provides the capacity and reliability your supply chain demands.

Why Truckload Brokerage Matters for Your Business

Freight markets fluctuate constantly. During tight capacity periods, shippers without brokerage relationships struggle to find trucks at any price. During soft markets, they overpay because they lack the carrier relationships and market intelligence to negotiate competitive rates. TCI's brokerage division solves both problems.

Our team monitors spot and contract market conditions daily, leveraging real-time rate data and lane-specific analytics to secure the best available capacity at fair market pricing. For shippers with seasonal surges, new product launches, or overflow beyond their dedicated fleet capacity, TCI's truckload brokerage provides the elastic capacity layer that keeps supply chains moving without long-term commitments.

TCI Approach

How TCI Delivers Truckload Brokerage Excellence

Every truckload shipment begins with TCI's load planning team matching your freight profile — commodity type, weight, dimensions, pickup and delivery requirements — against our carrier database to identify the optimal match. We prioritize carriers with proven performance on your specific lanes, verified safety records, and the right equipment for your cargo.

Once a carrier is assigned, TCI manages the shipment from pickup through delivery. Our tracking team monitors every load in real-time, providing proactive status updates and immediately escalating any exceptions. If a carrier issue arises — a breakdown, weather delay, or scheduling conflict — TCI's team has backup carriers ready to step in and protect your delivery commitment. You get a single point of contact, transparent pricing, and complete visibility from origin to destination.
