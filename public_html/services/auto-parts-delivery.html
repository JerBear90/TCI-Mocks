CDS — A TCI Company

OEM auto parts delivery nationwide.

After delivering auto parts in the western states for over 30 years, CDS has joined forces with TCI Transportation, a leader in the trucking industry with locations from coast to coast. This merger provides more robust, efficient, and reliable transportation solutions for auto parts delivery.

Auto Parts

From Manufacturers to Dealers

CDS has always been a niche carrier, focused solely on efficiently delivering new car replacement parts from manufacturers to dealers within dedicated service areas. In fact, CDS was the pioneer of the shared delivery system.

For safety and speed, all trailers are lift gate equipped to ensure timely deliveries even to dealers that don't have a dock receiving area. OEM parts are delivered 24/7, when and where you need them.

Just-In-Time

Just-In-Time Delivery Coast-to-Coast

Our "Just in time" delivery service means that you can keep minimal on-hand inventory, freeing up space and reducing costs. Instead, let us deliver the types and quantities of auto parts exactly when you need them.

A vast amount of our deliveries are done after hours, meaning your labor costs are reduced as well. Our expert drivers do not require delivery assistance, and with our lift-gate-equipped trailers, we'll get the job done.

Pioneer

We Pioneered the Shared Delivery System

CDS has been providing new car replacement parts for more than thirty years. We pioneered the shared delivery system, providing dedicated delivery at competitive rates.

CDS works directly with manufacturers, which means dealers can count on OEM parts being delivered quickly. Our carrier responsibility rate due to shorts or damaged parts is close to 0.1%, making our delivery success rate the envy of the industry.
