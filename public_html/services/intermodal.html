Intermodal Services

What is Intermodal?

Intermodal shipping refers to moving freight by two or more "modes" of transportation, using containers specifically designed to move cargo seamlessly between trucks, trains, and cargo ships.

Our extraordinary team of 45 drivers is helping fuel growth in our Intermodal division. In addition to our dedicated drivers, TCI features a service team that will get to know your business, becoming a seamless extension of your existing transportation department.

Sustainability

Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

The EPA reports that for shipments over 1,000 miles, using intermodal transport can cut fuel use and greenhouse gas emissions by as much as 65%, relative to truck transport alone.

A truck transporting 40,000 lbs from Boston to Los Angeles produces approximately 4.35 tons of carbon emissions. The same shipment via intermodal rail produces only 1.75 tons.
