Full-Service Leasing

24/7 truck roadside assistance that never leaves you stranded

Breakdowns don't wait for business hours. TCI Transportation's 24/7 truck roadside assistance is included with every full-service lease, providing immediate breakdown response, substitute vehicles, and nationwide coverage so your deliveries stay on schedule no matter what happens on the road.

Always On

What Is TCI's 24/7 Truck Roadside Assistance?

TCI's 24/7 truck roadside assistance is a comprehensive breakdown response program built into every full-service lease agreement. When a leased vehicle experiences a mechanical failure, tire blowout, electrical issue, or any other disabling event, your driver makes a single call to TCI's dedicated dispatch center — staffed around the clock, 365 days a year.

Our dispatch team immediately coordinates the fastest resolution, whether that means deploying a mobile repair technician to the breakdown location, arranging a tow to the nearest qualified repair facility, or dispatching a substitute vehicle to transfer the load and keep the delivery moving. There are no additional fees, no per-incident charges, and no coverage gaps — roadside assistance is a standard benefit of your TCI lease.

Zero Downtime

Why Roadside Assistance Is Critical for Fleet Operations

Every hour a truck sits disabled on the roadside costs your business money — in missed delivery windows, customer penalties, driver detention, and cargo spoilage for temperature-sensitive loads. Without a dedicated roadside assistance program, fleet managers scramble to find qualified repair shops in unfamiliar areas, negotiate emergency rates, and arrange alternative transportation.

TCI's roadside assistance eliminates that chaos. Our nationwide network of pre-vetted repair vendors means we already have relationships with qualified shops in every major corridor. Average response times run under 90 minutes for most locations, and our dispatch team manages the entire process from initial call through vehicle return to service. Your operations team stays focused on customers, not crisis management.

How TCI's Roadside Assistance Program Works

When a breakdown occurs, the driver calls TCI's 24/7 dispatch line. Our team captures the vehicle location, symptoms, and load details, then immediately determines the fastest path to resolution. For minor issues like tire failures or battery problems, we dispatch a mobile technician directly to the scene. For more complex repairs, we coordinate towing and shop scheduling simultaneously.

If the repair will take longer than your delivery timeline allows, TCI dispatches a substitute vehicle from the nearest location in our network. The load is transferred, the driver continues the route, and TCI manages the repair and return of the original vehicle. Every incident is tracked in our fleet management system, giving you complete visibility into breakdown history, response times, and repair costs — all included in your lease payment.
