Full-Service Leasing

Custom truck specifications leasing built for your operation

No two operations are alike, and your trucks shouldn't be either. TCI Transportation builds every leased vehicle to your exact specifications — from cargo vans and straight trucks to Class 8 tractors, Ottawa yard spotters, and electric vehicles — so your fleet performs precisely the way your business demands.

Spec-to-Order

What Is Custom Truck Specifications Leasing?

Custom truck specifications leasing means every vehicle in your TCI lease is purpose-built for your application. Rather than settling for off-the-lot inventory that almost fits your needs, TCI works with OEM manufacturers to configure each truck with the exact engine, transmission, axle ratio, wheelbase, cab configuration, and body type your operation requires.

Whether you need a refrigerated straight truck with a specific liftgate capacity, a day cab tractor with a particular fifth-wheel height, or an Ottawa yard spotter equipped for 24/7 terminal operations, TCI's spec engineers translate your operational requirements into a detailed build sheet that ensures every vehicle arrives ready to work from day one.

Right-Fit Equipment

Why Vehicle Customization Matters for Your Fleet

Generic trucks create operational compromises. An underpowered engine wastes fuel climbing grades. The wrong suspension damages cargo. An oversized vehicle can't navigate tight delivery routes. These mismatches cost money every single day through reduced efficiency, increased maintenance, and driver dissatisfaction.

TCI's custom truck specifications leasing eliminates these compromises. We match vehicle capabilities to your actual duty cycles, payload requirements, and route profiles. The result is lower fuel consumption, reduced wear on components, fewer breakdowns, and drivers who have the right tool for the job. Our clients typically see 8-12% improvement in fuel economy simply by right-sizing their spec'd equipment.

TCI Approach

How TCI Delivers Custom-Spec'd Lease Vehicles

TCI's vehicle customization process begins with an on-site operational assessment. Our spec engineers ride your routes, evaluate your loading docks, measure cargo dimensions, and interview your drivers to understand exactly what each vehicle needs to accomplish. We then develop detailed specifications covering powertrain, chassis, body, and auxiliary equipment.

Leveraging long-standing relationships with manufacturers like Freightliner, Peterbilt, Kenworth, Isuzu, and Ottawa, TCI secures priority build slots and competitive pricing. We manage the entire procurement process from order placement through final inspection and delivery. For specialized applications, we coordinate with body builders and upfitters to install custom racks, liftgates, refrigeration units, and telematics systems before the vehicle enters your fleet.
