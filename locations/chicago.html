Chicago, IL trucking & fleet services.

TCI Chicago provides transportation services including commercial truck rental and leasing, fleet maintenance, as well as local driver dedicated contract. The facility sits on a 7-acre lot in Country Club Hills just southeast of downtown. Conveniently located at the intersection of Interstates 80 and 57, it serves a number of industries in and around the states of Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, and Michigan including Food and Beverage, Manufacturing, Energy, and Retail.
