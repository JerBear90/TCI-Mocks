## What is Contract Maintenance?
Contract maintenance is an agreement between a business and a service provider where the business receives routine maintenance services for its fleet. TCI is proud to offer contract maintenance, meaning more businesses have access to the same great professionals and diligent maintenance protocols we use for our own equipment.

As more research comes out regarding the benefits of proactive and preventative maintenance, more companies are realizing the advantages of a contract maintenance program.

## How Do I Get Started?
At TCI, we believe every service should be customized to meet the needs of the business. That's why our contract maintenance services are fully tailored to the client based on their fleet size, equipment type, workload, and schedule.
