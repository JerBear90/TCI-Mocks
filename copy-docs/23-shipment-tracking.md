## What Is Shipment Tracking and Reporting?
Shipment tracking and reporting is the technology-driven process of monitoring freight movements in real time and generating actionable data from those movements. In a dedicated logistics operation, this means GPS-based vehicle tracking, electronic proof-of-delivery capture, automated status updates, and comprehensive performance reporting across your entire fleet.
TCI's shipment tracking and reporting platform provides your team with a single source of truth for all dedicated fleet activity. From pickup to final delivery, every milestone is captured, time-stamped, and available through customizable dashboards and automated reports that integrate with your existing systems.
## Why Shipment Tracking and Reporting Matters
In today's supply chain environment, customers and stakeholders expect real-time visibility into shipment status. Without robust tracking and reporting, logistics teams operate reactively, responding to customer inquiries with outdated information and making decisions based on incomplete data.
Comprehensive shipment tracking and reporting transforms your dedicated operation from reactive to proactive. Exception alerts notify your team of delays before customers call. Performance reports identify trends in on-time delivery, dwell times, and route efficiency that inform continuous improvement. The data captured through tracking also supports dispute resolution, billing accuracy, and regulatory compliance documentation.
## How TCI Delivers Tracking and Reporting Excellence
TCI equips every dedicated vehicle with GPS tracking and telematics technology that feeds real-time location and status data into our centralized management platform. Your team receives automated notifications at key milestones: dispatch, pickup, in-transit checkpoints, and delivery confirmation with electronic proof-of-delivery.
Beyond real-time tracking, TCI provides scheduled and on-demand reporting tailored to your KPIs. Standard reports cover on-time performance, miles driven, fuel consumption, and delivery exceptions. Custom reports can be configured to match your internal metrics, and data exports integrate seamlessly with your TMS, ERP, or business intelligence tools for end-to-end supply chain visibility.
