## What Is Fleet Equipment Maintenance?
Fleet equipment maintenance encompasses all preventative, scheduled, and corrective maintenance activities required to keep commercial vehicles and trailers operating safely and efficiently. In a dedicated logistics operation, this includes oil changes, brake inspections, tire management, engine diagnostics, DOT inspections, and emergency roadside repairs.
TCI's fleet equipment maintenance program is built around proactive service schedules that prevent breakdowns before they happen. Every vehicle in your dedicated fleet follows a customized maintenance plan based on mileage intervals, operating conditions, and manufacturer specifications, ensuring maximum uptime and extending equipment life.
## Why Fleet Equipment Maintenance Matters
Unplanned vehicle breakdowns are among the most expensive disruptions in fleet operations. A single roadside failure can cost thousands in emergency repairs, towing, missed deliveries, and customer penalties. For companies managing their own fleets, maintenance costs are unpredictable and often exceed budget projections.
TCI's approach to fleet equipment maintenance converts these variable costs into predictable, budgetable expenses. Our preventative maintenance programs reduce breakdown frequency, extend vehicle lifecycles, and ensure every truck in your dedicated fleet meets safety and compliance standards. Predictable costs mean no surprise invoices and more accurate transportation budgeting for your finance team.
## How TCI Maintains Your Dedicated Fleet
TCI operates maintenance facilities staffed with certified diesel technicians who service your dedicated fleet on a proactive schedule. Each vehicle is assigned a maintenance profile that tracks service history, upcoming intervals, and component lifecycle data. Our team uses this information to schedule service during off-peak hours, minimizing disruption to your delivery operations.
When unplanned repairs are needed, TCI's nationwide service network and 24/7 roadside assistance program ensure rapid response. Substitute vehicles are deployed to keep your deliveries on schedule while repairs are completed. All maintenance costs are bundled into your dedicated service agreement, giving you a single predictable monthly rate with no hidden charges.
