## What is LTL?
Less than Truckload (LTL) refers to shipments between 1-8 pallets commingling with other shippers' orders to create a more cost-efficient transaction utilizing the hub and spoke network.
Our customer obsessed TMS is customizable and great for driving business decisions for centralized and decentralized companies alike. Its primary functions include quoting, booking, tracking, tracing, and freight bill and audit.
