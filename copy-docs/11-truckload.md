## What is Truckload?
Truckload Capacity (or Full Truckload/FTL) shipping refers to the use of an entire truck for a shipment going from point A to point B. It differs from LTL as LTL shipments make multiple stops along a route. Truckload is the quickest method for delivering freight, typically large loads weighing more than 15,000 pounds.
Truckload Capacity has continued to come at a premium over this market cycle. As networks demand change, it has never been more important to have nimble transportation partners focused on delivering capacity solutions.
