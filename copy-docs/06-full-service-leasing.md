## What is Full Service Leasing?
Generally, a full service equipment lease is classified as a year or longer. The primary benefit is that it allows you to lock in a great rate today, so you have predictable business expenses in the future.

Compare that to a short-term rental, where you might get a new rate each time you come back to pick up equipment. A full service lease includes 24/7 roadside assistance, the latest fleet management technology, and substitute vehicles when your truck is down for repairs.

## Is a Full Service Lease Your Best Option?
The benefits of a full service lease are plentiful, especially for companies who expect a steady workload for the term of the lease. If you are working in a highly seasonal industry, you should consider a short-term rental instead.

Signing a full service lease with TCI is made to be simple and streamlined. The process starts with reviewing our available equipment and speaking to one of our friendly representatives about your needs.
