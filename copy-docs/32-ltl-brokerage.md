## What Is LTL Freight Brokerage?
LTL freight brokerage is a service that connects shippers with less-than-truckload carriers when their shipments don't require a full 53-foot trailer. Instead of paying for unused trailer space, LTL consolidates your pallets with freight from other shippers, and you pay only for the space your cargo occupies based on weight, dimensions, freight class, and distance.
TCI's LTL brokerage division maintains active partnerships with over 50 regional and national LTL carriers. When you submit a shipment, our system queries multiple carriers simultaneously to find the best combination of price, transit time, and service reliability for your specific lane. This multi-carrier approach consistently delivers rates 15-30% below what shippers negotiate on their own with individual carriers.
## Why LTL Brokerage Saves Your Business Money
Managing LTL shipping in-house means negotiating separate contracts with multiple carriers, tracking different rate structures and discount tiers, managing accessorial charges, and handling claims across different systems. Most shippers lack the volume to command the best rates from any single carrier, and the administrative burden of managing multiple carrier relationships drains resources.
TCI aggregates LTL volume across hundreds of shippers, giving us the purchasing power to negotiate deeply discounted rates that individual companies can't access. Our TMS integration means quotes flow directly into your existing systems — no manual data entry, no phone calls to carrier reps, no comparing rate sheets in spreadsheets. One request, multiple carrier options, instant booking, and consolidated invoicing that simplifies your accounts payable process.
## How TCI Manages Your LTL Freight
TCI's LTL brokerage starts with understanding your shipping profile — average shipment sizes, frequency, origin-destination pairs, and service requirements. We then configure our multi-carrier rating engine to prioritize the carriers that perform best on your specific lanes, balancing cost against transit time and damage-free delivery rates.
Once configured, your team can generate instant multi-carrier quotes through TCI's online portal or directly through your TMS via API integration. Booking is a single click. TCI generates BOLs, schedules pickups, and provides tracking updates through delivery. If a shipment is damaged or delayed, our claims team handles the entire process — filing, documentation, follow-up, and settlement — so your team never has to chase a carrier for resolution. Monthly reporting shows spend by carrier, lane, and service level so you can continuously optimize your LTL strategy.
