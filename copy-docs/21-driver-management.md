## What Is Dedicated Driver Management?
Dedicated driver management is a comprehensive workforce solution where TCI assumes full responsibility for every aspect of your driver operations. This includes recruiting qualified CDL holders, conducting thorough background checks, administering drug and alcohol testing, and onboarding drivers who meet both TCI's rigorous standards and your specific operational requirements.
Our driver management program covers the complete employment lifecycle: hiring, orientation, ongoing safety training, performance monitoring, compensation administration, benefits management, and day-to-day supervision. Your drivers wear your brand, serve your customers, and operate on your schedule, but TCI handles the HR complexity behind the scenes.
## Why Dedicated Driver Management Matters
The national driver shortage continues to challenge shippers and carriers alike. Recruiting, training, and retaining qualified commercial drivers requires specialized expertise, competitive compensation packages, and a workplace culture that keeps turnover low. For companies outside the transportation industry, building this capability in-house is costly and distracting.
TCI's dedicated driver management services solve this problem by leveraging decades of experience in driver recruitment and retention. Our established hiring pipelines, competitive pay structures, and driver-first culture result in lower turnover rates and more consistent service for your customers. You get a stable, professional driver workforce without the administrative burden.
## How TCI Manages Your Driver Workforce
TCI's dedicated driver management begins with understanding your operation's unique demands: route types, delivery windows, customer interaction requirements, and any specialized certifications needed. We then recruit drivers who match those criteria, put them through our comprehensive training program, and assign them exclusively to your account.
Our supervisory team monitors driver performance through telematics data, customer feedback, and regular ride-alongs. Compensation is structured to reward safety, efficiency, and tenure, which keeps our dedicated drivers engaged and committed to your business. TCI handles all payroll, benefits administration, workers' compensation, and HR compliance so your team never has to.
