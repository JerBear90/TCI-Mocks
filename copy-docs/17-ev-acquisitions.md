## Why Electrify with TCI?
The trucking industry produces about 37% of all emissions on highways in the United States. Zero-emissions vehicles are good for our environment, but some of the biggest benefits will affect your company directly.
Reduce operational costs by up to 70% when considering fuel and maintenance. Reduce tailpipe emissions by 100%. Significantly reduce noise emissions and promote sustainable transportation practices.
