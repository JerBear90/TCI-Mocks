## What is Freight Factoring?
Freight factoring is a financial solution tailored for trucking businesses that need immediate cash flow. By selling invoices to a factoring company for immediate payment at a slightly reduced rate, carriers avoid waiting the typical 30 to 90 days for customer payments.
This ensures steady cash flow to cover essential expenses such as fuel, maintenance, and payroll. Unlike traditional financing, freight factoring offers quicker processing times and fewer requirements.
